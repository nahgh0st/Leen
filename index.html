Whispers of the Desert

A Gulf-style Poetic Exploration

In the land where the sun kisses the dunes, And the stars weave tales of ancient tunes, The date palms sway, whispering soft lore, Of heartbeats echoing on the shore.

Footprints in sand, stories untold, In the embrace of the night, they unfold, The winds carry secrets from the sea, Harmonies of life, wild and free.

As the moon bathes the world in silver glow, Dreams dance lightly like waves that flow, Here in the warmth of the Gulf's embrace, Every soul finds its destined place.

The horizon blushes, painted anew, A canvas of colors, vibrant and true, Let the spirit of the Gulf guide your pen, To craft tales of beauty, again and again.
